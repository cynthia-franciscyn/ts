What is PPF

An Invisible
Physical Shield

Paint Protection Film is a thermoplastic urethane film bonded to your car's painted surfaces. Unlike ceramic coatings, PPF provides genuine physical impact protection — absorbing stone chips and scratches before they reach your paint.

9+ yrs
typical film lifespan in Bangalore

PPF takes the hit so your paint doesn't have to. Ceramic coatings have zero impact resistance. PPF provides complete impact protection and can be removed cleanly at end of life.

Why Bangalore

What Bangalore Roads
Do to Unprotected Paint

01
Construction Debris
Gravel and stone chips thrown up by trucks on Outer Ring Road and construction-heavy corridors cause paint chips on front bumpers and hoods daily.
02
Two-Wheeler Brushes
Bikes filtering through traffic make contact with door panels and mirrors constantly. These hairline scratches accumulate into visible damage within months.
03
Parking Hazards
Tight basement parking in tech parks and apartments across Bangalore means door dings, concrete pillar scrapes, and trolley scratches are near-inevitable.
04
Monsoon Debris
Waterlogged roads kick up high-velocity grit and debris that impacts lower panels, rocker panels, and rear bumpers during Bangalore's June–September season.
05
UV at Altitude
At 920 metres elevation, Bangalore's UV intensity is measurably higher than sea-level cities. PPF's UV blocker prevents paint oxidation and fading.
06
Bus & Truck Spray
BMTC buses and goods vehicles shed high-velocity road grit on Bangalore's ring roads, creating micro-impacts across entire front fascias over time.
Who Needs PPF

Is PPF Right for You?

NEW
New Car Owners
Protect from day one. A stone chip on a 3-week-old car hurts more than any service cost. PPF on delivery is the smartest time to install it.
LUX
Luxury & Sports Cars
Soft, show-quality paint on performance cars is the most vulnerable. Full body PPF is standard practice for BMWs, Porsches, and high-end SUVs.
ORR
Daily Highway Commuters
ORR, Hosur Road, and NICE Road at speed generate constant stone chip risk. Front protection package pays for itself in the first year.
WHT
Light Coloured Cars
Stone chips on white, silver, and cream paint are immediately visible and disproportionately impact resale value. PPF is essential.
EV
Electric Vehicles
EVs are high-value, often white or light-coloured, and driven at steady highway speeds. Full body PPF is becoming standard for Nexons, i4s, and Model 3s.
VAL
Resale-Minded Owners
Pristine factory paint under PPF commands a meaningful premium at resale. Buyers trust a car that has been properly protected from the start.
